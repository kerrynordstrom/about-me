General Duties

  • Create unique Bills of Materials (BOM) for each combination of color, size, and phase of production based on information received from product developers.
  • Maintain BOM integrity throughout the product lifecycle from Pre-Production Sample (PPS) through Salesman Sample (SMS), Size Run (SZR), and 1st Quality (1st).
  • Attend twice weekly meetings to receive style updates through all phases of production, from Pre-Production Sample (PPS) to 1st Quality production.
  • Create material and labor routing information in ERP system.
  • Build Labor Bill of Materials in piecework tracking system.
  • Maintain additional labor tracking tools including Leather Prep Order (LPO) and Zipper Prep Order (ZPO)
  • Report-as-Finished (RAF) and End production orders for registered goods in ERP system by end-of-month.
  • Provide labor costing with overhead to finance and product development team as requested.
